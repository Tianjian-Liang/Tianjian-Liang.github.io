Biography

Tianjian Liang is a Master student in Pharmaceutical Sciences at PITT, supervised by Dr. Zhiwei Feng. His research involves in-silico protein engineering, antibody design, and computational-aided drug discovery. These interests have led to projects proposed antibody design protocol, developed virus knowledge, and identified allosteric binding pocket of G proteins.

Virus-CKB: an integrated bioinformatics platform and analysis resource for COVID-19 research

Developed Virus-CKB for SARS-CoV-2 drug discovery. Found four potential FDA-proved antiviral drugs and three nonantiviral drugs which may target to 3CLpro of SARS-CoV-2. Proposed several of possible drugs combination to treat with COVID-19.

Binding Characterization of GPCRs-Modulator by Molecular Complex Characterizing System (MCCS)

Developed new algorithm called MCCS. Predicted possible allosteric binding site of CB2. Demonstrated MCCS program could predict potential small-molecule allosteric modulators for class A GPCRs.
